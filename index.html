About Us

Our Legacy

RSD College, Ferozpur City, is one of the oldest and most prestigious institutions of higher learning in Northern India. Founded in 1921, the college has been a beacon of light for the people of this border region.

Our mission is to provide quality education to all sections of society, especially the underprivileged, and to foster a spirit of inquiry and social responsibility among our students.

Vision & Mission

To be a center of excellence in education and research, producing globally competitive and socially responsible citizens who contribute to the progress of the nation.

Admissions

Admission Process 2024

  1. Fill out the online application form available on the portal.
  2. Upload required documents (10th, 12th Marksheets, Character Certificate).
  3. Pay the application processing fee online.
  4. Wait for the merit list announcement.
  5. Complete document verification and pay the admission fee.
